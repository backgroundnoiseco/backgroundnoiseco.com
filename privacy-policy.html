RNG - Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

RNG is developed by Background Noise LLC. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

RNG does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data generated and saved within the app (such as history and favorites) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

RNG does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at Background Noise LLC.
